# ContentStamp Chrome 商店上架素材

## 1. 插件描述 (英文)
```
ContentStamp — Timestamp your content on-chain. One click to prove you were first.

In the age of AI-generated content (DeepSeek, GPT, Claude), anyone can copy your work instantly. 
ContentStamp gives you an immutable, blockchain-backed timestamp proving when you created your content.

✨ HOW IT WORKS:
1. Browse any webpage
2. Right-click → "Stamp to ContentStamp" or click the extension icon
3. Your content hash gets stored on Base blockchain forever
4. Share the verification link — anyone can verify your timestamp

🔒 FEATURES:
• Right-click context menu — stamp page, selected text, links, or images
• One-click popup — full page content hashing with metadata
• Immutable proof — stored on Base L2 blockchain (gas ~$0.01)
• Public verification — anyone can verify at contentstamp.vercel.app
• Local history — view all your stamps right in the extension
• Free tier: 50 stamps/month, Pro: unlimited

🛡️ WHY BLOCKCHAIN?
Blockchain timestamps cannot be forged, backdated, or deleted. 
Unlike screenshots or server logs, on-chain proof is mathematically verifiable by anyone.

🌐 Base L2 means low gas fees (~$0.01 per stamp) and fast confirmation.
Works with MetaMask and any Ethereum-compatible wallet.
```
